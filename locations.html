Locations

Tropixel is located in Toronto. We have two locations - High Seas and Sunnyscape.

Want to know which location you're closest to?

High Seas

High Seas is our main location. It opened in 2012 and continues to be our busiest spot. We are open 7 days a week with extended hours on Friday and Saturday. Weekend reservations usually fill up fast especially during our busy seasons so be sure to book your dinner parties early.
