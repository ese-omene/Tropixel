About Us

Located in Toronto, Tropixel is the #1 spot to come and hang out and enjoy a delicious lunch or dinner. We might be in the heart of the city but it always feels like your on the beach when you come out to eat at our place. We take pride in offering great quality food in a relaxed casual atmosphere. We make everything from scratch using only the freshest ingredients from local suppliers and Ontario farms. Our food is always fresh, unique and full of flavour.

The Owners

Savannah and Kevin

Savannah and Kevin are no strangers to a good meal. They are award winning chefs with years of experience working in the restaurant industry. They were both looking for a new challenge so they decided to venture out on their own and open Tropixel in 2012. It was a decision with it's fair share of challenges and they didnt have all the answers in the beginning but they knew one thing - they love food and wanted to make their customers happy with delicious affordable meals and a fun atmosphere full of good vibes. They are happy to welcome you to their "chill spot" where you can eat, drink and hang out with your favourite people.
